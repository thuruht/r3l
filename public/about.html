Project Philosophy

R3L:F (Relational Ephemeral Filenet) is an anti-algorithmic social file-sharing platform designed to put human connection back at the center of online interaction. We prioritize intentional sharing over algorithmic optimization, meaningful connections over viral engagement, and user control over corporate manipulation.

Our approach stands in contrast to traditional social networks by:

  • Making content ephemeral by default to keep communities fresh and relevant
  • Using explicit, mutual connections rather than one-sided follows
  • Prioritizing file sharing as the foundation of interaction
  • Ensuring chronological ordering and user-controlled filtering instead of engagement-optimized feeds
  • Visualizing connections in an intuitive association web

Core Concepts

Relational

The "R" in R3L:F stands for the relationship-centered approach. Users connect through explicit, mutually agreed relationships rather than one-sided follows. These connections are visualized in the association web, showing the strength and nature of relationships.

Ephemeral

The "E" in R3L:F represents the ephemeral nature of content. By default, content expires after 7 days unless it's archived by community consensus. This ensures that the platform remains fresh and relevant, rather than accumulating forgotten content indefinitely.

Filenet

The "F" in R3L:F stands for Filenet - a social network where files (documents, images, audio, video, and creative works) are the foundation of interaction. Content is the focus, not likes or shallow engagement metrics.

Project History

R3L:F was conceived as an alternative to algorithm-driven social platforms that optimize for engagement at the expense of user well-being and meaningful connection. The project began as a proof of concept exploring how social platforms might be designed differently if they prioritized human connection over algorithmic optimization.

The first prototype was developed in mid-2024, with a focus on creating a minimalist interface that emphasized file sharing and direct connection. The current version represents an evolution of those early ideas, with enhanced features for visualization, ephemeral content management, and community archiving.

Technology Stack

R3L:F is built using modern web technologies with a focus on performance, security, and scalability:

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Vanilla)
  • Backend: Cloudflare Workers (Edge computing)
  • Database: Cloudflare D1 (SQLite at the edge)
  • File Storage: Cloudflare R2 (S3-compatible object storage)
  • Real-time Features: Cloudflare Durable Objects
  • Visualization: D3.js for association web
  • Maps: Leaflet.js for geographic visualization

The platform is designed to be lightweight and efficient, with most processing happening at the edge to ensure low latency and high availability worldwide.

Future Roadmap

R3L:F is under active development with several exciting features planned for future releases:

  • Enhanced content archiving with community voting mechanisms
  • Improved file type support and preview capabilities
  • Advanced privacy controls and granular sharing options
  • Enhanced association web with thematic clustering
  • Additional customization options for communiques
  • Mobile applications for iOS and Android

We're committed to maintaining the core philosophy of the platform while expanding its capabilities to meet the needs of our community.
